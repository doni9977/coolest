Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

1. General Provisions

This Privacy Policy explains how Colo.ai Technologies processes and protects information received from users of the Colo.ai website and service. By using the website and service, you confirm your agreement with this Policy.

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of data: contact details of organization representatives (name, title, work email, phone number, institution name), information submitted through the demo request form, technical website visit data (IP address, device type, browser, cookies and similar technologies), as well as study data uploaded for analysis under a contract.

3. Medical Data

When working with medical images and related data, we apply a data minimization principle. Unless otherwise provided by contract or applicable law, patient data must be transferred in de-identified or anonymized form. The user transferring the data is responsible for ensuring a valid legal basis for that transfer.

4. Purposes of Data Processing

We process data to provide access to the service and its features, handle incoming requests and feedback, organize product demonstrations, fulfill contractual obligations, improve service quality and security, comply with legal requirements, and maintain internal reporting.

5. Legal Bases for Processing

Legal bases for processing may include the data subject's consent, performance of a contract or steps prior to entering into one, compliance with legal obligations, and the legitimate interest of the controller provided the rights and freedoms of data subjects are respected.

6. Sharing Data with Third Parties

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared with third parties only where necessary for service operation, such as cloud infrastructure, technical support, or hosting providers, under confidentiality and data processing agreements, or when required by law.

7. Cross-Border Transfer

If cross-border data transfer is required to provide the service, we apply the safeguards required by applicable law, including contractual guarantees and technical and organizational protection measures.

8. Retention Periods

We retain data no longer than necessary to achieve processing purposes, fulfill contractual obligations, and comply with legal requirements. After retention periods expire, data is deleted or anonymized unless otherwise required by law.

9. Security

We apply a combination of organizational and technical security measures, including access controls, encryption in transit and at rest (where applicable), activity logging, backups, and incident monitoring. Even so, no method of data transmission over the internet guarantees absolute security.

10. Data Subject Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict processing of, and transfer your data, as well as the right to withdraw consent and lodge a complaint with a competent authority. To exercise these rights, send a request using the contact details below.

11. Cookies and Analytics

The website may use cookies and similar technologies for proper operation, analytics, and improvement of user experience. You may change cookie settings in your browser, but this may affect the operation of certain site features.

12. Changes to the Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The current version is always published on this page with the date of the latest update.
