Problem

Colorectal cancer is the second deadliest in the world, yet much of it can be prevented

The tragedy is not the disease itself. It is how often it remains unnoticed until it is too late. Current screening tools still miss far too much.

2M

new colorectal cancer cases are diagnosed globally every year

60%

of cases are detected at late stages, when survival drops significantly

1 in 4

polyps are missed during standard colonoscopy

Our Solution

Colo.ai analyzes endoscopic images in seconds

Our AI detects early signs of cancer within seconds after study data is uploaded. Fast and accurate validation without wasting clinical time.

Polyp Detection

Detects polyps as small as 1 mm with neural-network precision, catching what the human eye can miss.

Risk Assessment

Classifies findings within seconds as low, medium, or high risk, giving physicians a clear signal for action.

Image Analysis

The system processes uploaded endoscopic images in seconds, highlighting polyps and suspicious areas for rapid physician validation.

Automatic Reports

Structured reports are generated in seconds and integrated directly into the patient's electronic medical record.
